About Kala Props

A collector's eye,
run like a studio.

KALA PROPS is a props house built one piece at a time — hand-painted ceramics, tarnished brass, silverware and vintage tableware, sourced the way a collector shops rather than the way a warehouse restocks.

Every item that leaves our Goregaon warehouse has already been photographed, catalogued and checked over, so what you see is exactly what arrives on set — wrapped, on time, and ready to be returned once the shoot wraps.
