About Kala Props

A design studio
that happens to
rent furniture.

KALA PROPS LLP began as a props house for Mumbai's film industry and grew into a full-scale furniture rental studio for homes, offices and events. Every piece that leaves our warehouse has been chosen the way a set designer chooses a prop — for character, finish and fit.

Today we run dedicated warehouses in Mumbai and Hyderabad, a fleet for white-glove delivery, and an in-house styling team that treats every drop-off like a final walkthrough.
